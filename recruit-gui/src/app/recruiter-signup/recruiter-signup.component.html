Free trial

IT Recruiters are offered a free 30 day trial with full access to the system. Within this time you have full access the CV database and can post open roles on the IT job board.

After the trial period has expired you can still access the site using the free credit based system. You will be assigned a set amount of credits each week. Once the credits run out you will have to wait until you have new credits to carry out certain function on the site

Alternatively you can opt for a paid subscription. With subscription based access there are no limits to how many functions you can carry out on the site.

What is Arenella-ICT

Arenella is a platorm for recruiters to connect with available IT candidates. Via Arenella recruiters can search on and download the CVs and contact details of available IT candidates. Recruiters using Arenella can also place job adverts to connect with candidates not currently in the system

Our background in IT

Having spent over 22 years working in the IT sector we have extensive knowledge of the IT markets in which we work. Since 2021 Arenella has turned its attention to sourcing of candidates for the IT recruitment sector.

IT candidate CV database

The IT candidate CV database is one of the main features of the site. We actively search for available IT candidates and with their permission add their CV and profile into the system. As a recruiter you can filter on your exact requirements and download the CVs of suitable candidates.

We source both contract and permanent IT candidates for the following locations
  • The Netherlands
  • Belgium
  • The United Kingdom
  • The republic of Ireleand

All candidates are local, hand picked and either native or have valid work visas.

Our ICT candidates speak diverse languages, including Dutch, English and French

Is the trial completely free

The 90 day trial is completely free. You have full access to the features of the site. There are no hidden costs or margins. You can place as many candidates as you like during the trial and there are no costs involved

What happens at the end of the 90 day trial period

At the end of the 90 day trial period your account blocks automatically. If you no longer wish to use the site there is no action required. Therefore there is no risk to taking out the trial.

Can I use the site after the 90 day free trial

Yes. Once the trial ends your account is automatically blocked. When you log-in you will see an option to take out an annual subscription to continue using the site. This subscription costs 120 euro's per year excluding tax. Once your request has been made you will be contacted for the details to setup the invoice.

Are there additional costs

No. The only cost is the annual subscription. You have full access to the site all the time you have a subscription. There are no margins or hidden fees

Where will the invoice for the annual subscription come from

The invoice is sent from Arenella BV a Dutch registered company in the Netherlands

Can I contact the candidates directly

Yes. When you view / download the candidates CV you will see their contact details. You can call them directly

Do candidates know their details are being shared

Yes. All candidates have either uploaded their CVs themselves via the site with the intention of having them downloaded by recruiters who will call them or have given permission to have them uploaded on their behalf for the same reason. We do not add any CV that has not been expressly provided to us for this purpose

Are the candidates actually available

Yes. Candidates availability is checked based upon their LinkedIn status roughly every two weeks. Providing their statuses are up to date the majority of candidates are actively looking for a new role

What countries are the candidates located in

There are candidates available in the following countries

  • The Netherlands
  • Belgium
  • United Kingdom
  • The Republic Of Ireland

You can filter on candidates that speak the language you are looking for

Are the candidates local

Yes. The candidates are living in the county where they are looking for work and are either nationals of that country or have a valid work visa.

What languages do the candidates speak

When a candidate is registered we make a note of their ability with the following languages

  • English
  • Dutch
  • French

You can filter on candidates that speak the language you are looking for
